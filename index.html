DreadfulFish

It's simple, select either you want to shorten your text or expand it.

DreadfulFish.

Written too much? Too little? DreadfulFish will grow or shrink any text you throw at it. Single words are replaced with phrases, or phrases with single words, so the length changes but your meaning stays the same. If you are under a character restriction (or maybe you are trying to reach those 1500 words) then you can use DreadfulFish to either shorten or expand your tweet,text or your essay. Simples.

This web app was "developed" during the "OpenSauce Hackathon" at Cardiff University; for more details visit cs.cf.ac.uk/hackathon
